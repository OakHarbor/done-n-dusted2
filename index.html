Cleaning FAQ

Frequently asked questions about our services.

How much does a cleaning service cost?

The average house cleaning cost is $25 - $65 per hour, per maid. The more popular maid services charge more than $60 per hour per maid. It really depends on how much work you want done, if you need specialty services, square footage, etc.

How to choose a cleaning service

We know choosing a cleaning company cna be a hassle and often have no idea where to begin. The number 1 rule you should abide by is to interview at least 3 companies before making a decision. You want to make sure you find the one that fits you and your home. When calling for references, ask about the companies punctuality, their cleaning ability, attitude and work ethic, and how much they trust them. After all, these people will be in your home! You want to know as much as you can about how they operate.
